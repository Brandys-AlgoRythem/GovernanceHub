How The Hive Works

  1. FoundationStart with the workspace profile, ownership model, records, contacts, and governance cadence.
  2. ObligationsTrack deadlines, reviews, renewals, owners, jurisdiction, priority, and required proof.
  3. ControlsMap SOPs and controls to risks, obligations, owners, cadence, and evidence requirements.
  4. EvidenceLink proof records back to obligations, documents, licenses, expenses, and controls.
  5. ReadinessUse the dashboard to see what is healthy, what is missing, and what needs attention next.
