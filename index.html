How The Workspace Works

  1. FoundationStart with the governance profile, ownership model, records, contacts, and governance cadence.
  2. Compliance CalendarTrack deadlines, reviews, renewals, owners, jurisdiction, priority, and required proof.
  3. Control LibraryMap SOPs and controls to risks, obligations, owners, cadence, and evidence requirements.
  4. Evidence CenterLink proof records back to obligations, documents, licenses, expenses, and controls.
  5. ReadinessUse the executive overview to see what is healthy, what is missing, and what needs attention next.
