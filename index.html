Business GRC Coverage

Built around the core things a business GRC workspace needs.

Governance

Entity profile, ownership, roles, records, operating cadence, approvals, continuity, and executive visibility.

Risk

Operational risks, vendor risks, documentation gaps, continuity exposure, mitigation, owners, and residual risk.

Compliance

Obligations, licenses, renewals, tax support, insurance reviews, evidence mapping, and audit-ready proof.

Operating Workflow

  1. FoundationCapture entity profile, owners, roles, agent details, and formation records.
  2. CalendarTrack obligations, licenses, permits, renewals, reviews, and filing owners.
  3. ControlsMap internal rules, SOPs, review cadence, required proof, and risk ownership.
  4. EvidenceConnect documents, receipts, renewal proof, policies, and records before anyone asks.
  5. ReadinessReview health, open risks, missing records, continuity gaps, and next actions.
