What it does

Turns business chaos into governance visibility.

Governance Hub models how a small business can track entity records, obligations, controls, evidence, expenses, and operational dependencies before audit season arrives wearing steel-toe boots.

Core Modules

Executive Dashboard

Governance health, deadlines, risk, evidence coverage, and recent activity.

Business Profile

Entity summary, owners, registered agent, contacts, vendors, and records.

Obligations Tracker

State filings, renewals, tax reminders, insurance dates, and operating reviews.

Governance Vault

Formation documents, SOPs, policies, licenses, confirmations, and proofs.

Risk Register

Operational, compliance, vendor, documentation, and continuity risks.

Evidence Tracker

Maps proof artifacts to obligations, controls, and audit support needs.

Governance Workflow

  1. FoundationCapture entity profile, roles, ownership, and formation records.
  2. ControlTrack obligations, SOPs, licenses, permits, and renewal owners.
  3. EvidenceLink documents and proof to requirements before someone asks for them.
  4. IntelligenceSurface deadlines, open risks, continuity gaps, and roadmap priorities.

Portfolio Scope
