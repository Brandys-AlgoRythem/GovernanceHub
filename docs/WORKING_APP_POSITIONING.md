# Working App Positioning

Governance Hub should be positioned as a working local-first business GRC workspace with sample data.

It is not just a static demo page.

A reviewer can:

1. Open the landing page.
2. Enter dummy business information.
3. Save the sample workspace in the browser.
4. Open the dashboard.
5. Review obligations, documents, licenses, risks, controls, evidence, expenses, and roadmap items.

## How to Describe It

Use this framing:

Governance Hub is a local-first business GRC workspace that lets a reviewer enter sample business details and explore how a small business could organize governance, risk, compliance, evidence, records, vendors, expenses, and audit readiness.

## What It Covers

The workspace covers:

- Business foundation
- Ownership and roles
- Obligations and renewals
- Governance records
- Licenses and permits
- Risk register
- Controls and SOPs
- Evidence tracker
- Expense support
- Vendor dependencies
- Continuity review
- Audit readiness
- Executive reporting

## Boundary

The app uses sample data and browser storage.

It should not claim to manage real compliance for a live business.

It should show how a comprehensive business GRC system could work, how the modules connect, and how the product could grow.
